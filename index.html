  • Tanja Simonič Korošak PhD

    Sem pooblaščena krajinska arhitektka (ZAPS 1623) in konservatorka (MK) in izdelujem načrte krajinske arhitekture, oblikujem različne produkte in izvajam delavnice skiciranja v Studiu TSK, Oblikovanje krajine Tanja Simonič Korošak s.p. v Mariboru, Slovenija. Poleg načrtovalskega dela tudi poučujem; na BOKU na Dunaju, na Univerzi v Mariboru in na Visoku šoli za trajnostni turizem v Ljubljani. Sem doktorica in magistra krajinske arhitekture, študirala sem v Sloveniji v Angliji in na Švedskem. Tako oblikovalsko kot raziskovlano me zanimajo praksa in teorija krajinskega oblikvoanja, zgodovina krajinskega oblikovanja in načrtovalsko mišljenje. Preprična sem v formativni pomen prostoročnega skiciranja izvajam delavnice prostoročnega krajinskega in urbanega skiciranja. Živim in delam v Mariboru. | I am a practicing licenced landscape arhitect at my Studio TSK in Maribor, Slovenia. I teach at BOKU University Vienna Austria, at University of Maribor Slovenia and at Erudio College for sustainable tourism in Ljubljana. I studied in Slovenia, UK and Sweden. I hold a PhD nad Masters degree in landscape architecture, as well as Certificate in landscape studies from Sheffiled University UK. I hold a BSc in Biology. My fields of design and research interest are landscape design practice and theory, history of landscape design and design thinking. My great passion is drawing by thinking hand and I conduct workshops in freehand landscape and urban sketching and drawing. I live and work in Maribor, Slovenia.

Škofja Loka historic centre revitalisation

Landscape architecture / team Architectural office Ravnikar Potokar Ljubljana

Redesign and revitalisation of the urban spaces, squares and parks of one of the oldest slovene towns Škofja loka implemented holistic approach of subtle redefinition and exploration of existing materials, landscape compositions and structures in order to create liveable and modern urban spaces which are closely related to the historic urban substance. 2015 Golden Pencil Award - Slovenian Chamber of architecture and spatial planning of Slovenia.

  • Date: 2013 Competition Winner / 2014 partial construction of Cankarjev trg and Blževa ulica / 2015 Nomination for Plečnik Prize / 2015 Golden Pencil Award - Slovenian Chamber of architecture and spatial planning of Slovenia
  • Client: Municipality of Škofja Loka / Architectural office Ravnikar Potokar Ljubljana
