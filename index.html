Škofja Loka historic centre revitalisation

Landscape architecture / team Architectural office Ravnikar Potokar Ljubljana

Redesign and revitalisation of the urban spaces, squares and parks of one of the oldest slovene towns Škofja loka implemented holistic approach of subtle redefinition and exploration of existing materials, landscape compositions and structures in order to create liveable and modern urban spaces which are closely related to the historic urban substance. 2015 Golden Pencil Award - Slovenian Chamber of architecture and spatial planning of Slovenia.

  • Date: 2013 Competition Winner / 2014 partial construction of Cankarjev trg and Blževa ulica / 2015 Nomination for Plečnik Prize / 2015 Golden Pencil Award - Slovenian Chamber of architecture and spatial planning of Slovenia
  • Client: Municipality of Škofja Loka / Architectural office Ravnikar Potokar Ljubljana
